Poetry and Place: Chicago (PPC)

A digital repository designed to highlight Chicago through poets, their respective poetry, and the locative elements that are the subjects of their works. Its purpose through digitized poetry and images is to educate and enlighten students, educators, and general audiences on the literary rich and familiar city, Chicago.
