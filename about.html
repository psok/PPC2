About

Poetry and Place: Chicago (PPC) is a digital repository designed to highlight Chicago through poets, their respective poetry, and the locative elements that are the subjects of their works. Its purpose through digitized poetry and images is to educate and enlighten students, educators, and general audiences on the literary rich and familiar city, Chicago.

It will largely markup selected representative poetry of major and minor Chicago poets while also immersing audiences in the visual elements of photography, video, and interactive tours that give rise to the literary content. As a mostly informative website, PPC will allow users to search and discover the literary art of a poet in the form of written texts, video, and sound recordings. It will also match a particular poet with a visual element of Chicago to bring alive the poets and their work. Overall, PPC will emerge as one interactive literary web tool that teaches and enlightens through the literary, visual, and interactive.
